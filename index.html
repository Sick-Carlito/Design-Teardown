Jen Looper describes herself as a multilingual multiculturalist with a passion for hardware hacking, mobile apps, Vue.js, machine learning, and discovering new things every day. With over 18 years’ experience as a web and mobile developer and specializing in creating cross-platform mobile apps, she is a Google Developer Expert and a Cloud Developer Advocate Lead at Microsoft. To promote diversity in the Vue.js community, Jen founded Vue Vixens, an initiative that hosts worldwide workshops and meetups for people who identify as women.
